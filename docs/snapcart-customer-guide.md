# Page 1: Detailed Refund, Return & Replacement Policy

## Eligibility Criteria for Returns

Not all items qualify for a return. Before initiating a return, confirm that your item meets **all** of the following conditions:

- The item was purchased directly from SnapCart (not a third-party marketplace seller unless their listing explicitly states SnapCart-backed returns).
- The return request is initiated within the applicable return window (measured from the delivery date stamped in your order tracking, not the order placement date).
- The item is in the condition required for its category (see category-specific rules below).
- The item is not in the non-returnable exclusion list.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items **cannot** be returned or refunded under any circumstances:

- Gift cards and store credit vouchers.
- Downloadable or digital products (e-books, software license keys).
- Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance — No Returns" on the product listing at the time of purchase.
- Personalized or custom-engraved products.
- Intimate apparel, swimwear, and undergarments (for hygiene reasons).
- Hazardous materials, flammable liquids, and pressurized containers.
- Items that have been physically altered, modified, or repaired by the customer or a third party.

## Replacement vs. Refund — How We Decide

When you file a return, SnapCart will offer you a choice between a **replacement** and a **refund** when both options are available. Here is how each option works:

| Scenario | Replacement Available? | Refund Available? |
|---|---|---|
| Item is in stock and you want the same product | Yes — shipped within 2 business days of receiving return | Yes |
| Item is out of stock | No — refund automatically issued | Yes |
| Item was a limited-edition or seasonal product | No | Yes |
| Defective item (manufacturer fault) | Yes — expedited priority shipping at no cost | Yes — plus return shipping reimbursed |
| Wrong item shipped (SnapCart error) | Yes — correct item shipped immediately via express | Yes — full refund including original shipping fees |

## Partial Refunds

A partial refund (rather than a full refund) may be issued in the following cases:

- The returned item shows signs of use beyond reasonable inspection (e.g., scuff marks on shoes that were worn outdoors, visible stains on clothing).
- Accessories, manuals, or original packaging are missing. A deduction of up to 15% of the item price may apply.
- The item is returned outside the standard window but within a courtesy extension granted by a customer service agent. A 20% restocking fee may apply.

## Refund Not Received — Detailed Troubleshooting

If you have been told your refund has been processed but you have not received it, follow these steps in order:

1. **Check the refund timeline.** Credit/debit card refunds can take up to 10 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your bank. The 3-5 business day estimate begins when SnapCart processes the refund, not when your bank posts it.
2. **Look for the correct line item.** Some banks post refunds as a separate credit rather than reversing the original charge. Search your statement for a credit from "SNAPCART" or "SC*SNAPCART."
3. **Check your original payment method.** Refunds are always returned to the original payment method used at checkout. If you paid with a debit card linked to a checking account, the refund goes to that checking account — not to a different card or account.
4. **Contact your bank or card issuer.** If 10 business days have passed since SnapCart confirmed the refund and you still do not see it, call the number on the back of your card. Provide them with the SnapCart refund reference number (found in your refund confirmation email) and ask them to trace the transaction.
5. **File a refund inquiry with SnapCart.** If your bank cannot locate the refund, contact SnapCart support with:
   - Your order ID
   - The refund confirmation email or reference number
   - A screenshot of your bank statement covering the expected refund period
   - Your bank's response or case number from Step 4

   SnapCart will investigate and, if necessary, re-issue the refund or provide an alternative payment method.

## Return Shipping — Who Pays?

- **Defective/damaged/wrong items:** SnapCart pays all return shipping. A prepaid label is emailed to you.
- **Change of mind / buyer's remorse (standard returns):** SnapCart provides free return shipping labels for all domestic returns within the contiguous United States.
- **Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. territories:** A flat $7.99 return shipping fee is deducted from your refund for change-of-mind returns. Defective item returns remain free.
- **International returns:** Not currently supported. International customers should contact support for case-by-case assistance.

## Refund Method Exceptions

- If your original credit or debit card has expired, been cancelled, or replaced since the purchase, the refund will still be routed to the same account by your card issuer. Contact your bank if you are unsure.
- PayPal refunds are returned to your PayPal balance, not to the underlying funding source (bank account or card) within PayPal.
- If you paid using a promotional gift card, the refund is issued as SnapCart store credit, not back to the gift card.

# Page 2: Shipping Partners & Carrier Information

## Our Shipping Carrier Network

SnapCart partners with multiple logistics providers to ensure reliable, fast delivery across the United States. The carrier assigned to your order depends on the shipping method selected, the package dimensions, and your delivery region.

### Primary Carriers

| Carrier | Services Used | Typical Use Cases |
|---|---|---|
| **UPS (United Parcel Service)** | UPS Ground, UPS 2nd Day Air, UPS Next Day Air | Standard and express shipments nationwide; packages over 1 lb |
| **FedEx** | FedEx Home Delivery, FedEx 2Day, FedEx Priority Overnight | Express and next-day orders; high-value electronics shipments |
| **USPS (United States Postal Service)** | USPS Priority Mail, USPS First-Class Package | Lightweight items (books, small accessories); P.O. Box deliveries |
| **OnTrac** | OnTrac Ground, OnTrac Sunrise | Same-day and next-day deliveries in select West Coast metros (LA, SF, Portland, Seattle) |
| **LSO (Lone Star Overnight)** | LSO Priority, LSO Economy | Next-day and same-day deliveries in Texas and surrounding states |

### Regional Last-Mile Partners

For same-day delivery in select metropolitan areas, SnapCart also works with local last-mile delivery services:

- **New York City metro:** Delivered by **AxleHire** or **Veho** couriers.
- **Los Angeles metro:** Delivered by **OnTrac** or **AxleHire** couriers.
- **Chicago metro:** Delivered by **Veho** or **LSO** couriers.
- **San Francisco Bay Area:** Delivered by **OnTrac** couriers.

These last-mile partners provide real-time GPS tracking and photo confirmation of delivery.

## Tracking Your Shipment Across Carriers

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing:

- The carrier name (e.g., "UPS," "FedEx")
- A tracking number hyperlinked to the carrier's tracking page
- Estimated delivery date

You can also track directly on the carrier's website:

| Carrier | Tracking URL |
|---|---|
| UPS | ups.com/track |
| FedEx | fedex.com/tracking |
| USPS | tools.usps.com/go/TrackConfirmAction |
| OnTrac | ontrac.com/tracking |
| LSO | lso.com/track |

> **Tip:** Tracking numbers may take 12–24 hours to become active in the carrier's system after you receive the shipping confirmation email. If the tracking page shows "Not Found," wait and try again.

## Signature Requirements

- Orders valued at **$200 or more** require a signature upon delivery. The carrier will make up to 3 delivery attempts. After 3 failed attempts, the package is returned to SnapCart and a full refund is issued.
- Orders under $200 are delivered without a signature unless you request one at checkout (where available).
- You may authorize a "safe place" delivery by leaving specific delivery instructions in your account's shipping address (e.g., "Leave at side door" or "Leave with front desk").

## Shipping Cut-Off Times

| Shipping Method | Order By (Local Time) | Ships Same Day? |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | 2:00 PM ET | Yes (Mon–Fri) |
| Express | 2:00 PM ET | Yes (Mon–Fri) |
| Next Day | 1:00 PM ET | Yes (Mon–Fri) |
| Same Day | 12:00 PM (Noon) Local | Yes (7 days/week in eligible metros) |

Orders placed after cut-off or on weekends/holidays (for non-same-day methods) ship the next business day.

## Shipping Delays — What To Do

If your package is delayed beyond the estimated delivery date:

1. Check the carrier tracking page for status updates. Common delay reasons include weather events, carrier facility backlogs, and incorrect address information.
2. If tracking shows "Delivery Exception" or "Address Issue," contact SnapCart support immediately — we can work with the carrier to redirect the package or update the address.
3. If tracking has not updated for **5 or more business days**, contact SnapCart support. We will open a carrier investigation (also called a "tracer") which typically resolves within 3–5 business days.
4. If the investigation confirms the package is lost, SnapCart will either re-ship the order at no cost or issue a full refund — your choice.

## Package Theft and "Porch Piracy"

If you believe your delivered package was stolen:

1. Check with neighbors and household members, and look around the delivery area (side doors, behind planters, with building management).
2. If the carrier provided a delivery photo, review it to confirm the drop-off location.
3. File a police report with your local police department. You will need the report number.
4. Contact SnapCart support within **48 hours** of the delivery timestamp with:
   - Your order ID
   - The police report number
   - A brief description of the situation

   SnapCart will review the claim and, as a one-time courtesy per account, either re-ship the order or issue a refund. Repeated theft claims on the same account may require additional verification.

# Page 3: Selling on SnapCart — Marketplace Seller Guide

## Overview

SnapCart's Marketplace program allows approved third-party sellers to list and sell products alongside SnapCart's own inventory. As a seller, you gain access to SnapCart's established customer base, fulfillment infrastructure, and trusted brand reputation.

## Who Can Sell on SnapCart?

SnapCart Marketplace is open to:

- **Businesses** registered in the United States with a valid EIN (Employer Identification Number) or equivalent state business registration.
- **Sole proprietors** with a valid SSN/ITIN and a registered business name (DBA).
- **International sellers** with a U.S.-based entity, bank account, and a domestic return address.

SnapCart does not currently support individual (non-business) sellers or consignment-style listings.

## How to Apply

1. **Visit the Seller Portal** at sellers.snapcart.com and click "Apply to Sell."
2. **Complete the application form**, providing:
   - Legal business name and DBA (if applicable)
   - Business address and contact information
   - Tax identification number (EIN or SSN)
   - Bank account details for deposit payments (U.S. bank required)
   - Brief description of the products you intend to sell
   - Links to your existing online store or product catalog (if any)
3. **Upload required documentation:**
   - Government-issued photo ID of the primary account holder
   - Business license or registration certificate
   - W-9 tax form (for U.S. entities)
4. **Submit the application.** SnapCart's Seller Verification team reviews applications within 5–10 business days. You will receive an email notification with your approval status.

## Seller Fees and Commission Structure

| Fee Type | Amount | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly subscription | $29.99/month | Access to Seller Central dashboard, analytics, and listing tools. Waived for the first 60 days. |
| Referral fee (commission) | 8%–15% per sale | Varies by product category. Electronics: 8%. Clothing: 12%. Home goods: 10%. Books: 15%. |
| Payment processing fee | 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction | Covers credit card and payment gateway processing. |
| Fulfillment by SnapCart (FBS) | Variable | Optional program. Fees based on item size and weight. See the FBS Rate Card in Seller Central for details. |

## Listing Your Products

Once approved, you can list products through Seller Central:

1. **Create a listing** by entering: product title, description, category, price, inventory quantity, and up to 8 product images.
2. **Follow SnapCart's listing standards:**
   - Titles must be factual and not include promotional language ("BEST DEAL!" or "LIMITED TIME!").
   - Descriptions must accurately represent the product. Misleading claims are grounds for listing removal.
   - Images must be high-resolution (minimum 1000×1000 px), on a white background for the primary image, and must not contain watermarks or promotional text overlays.
3. **Set your shipping options.** You may use your own shipping or enroll in Fulfillment by SnapCart (FBS), where SnapCart stores, packs, and ships your products from our warehouses.
4. **Publish the listing.** New listings undergo a 24–48-hour review before going live.

## Fulfillment by SnapCart (FBS)

FBS is an optional program where SnapCart handles storage, packing, shipping, and customer service for your products.

**How FBS Works:**

1. Ship your inventory to a designated SnapCart fulfillment center.
2. SnapCart stores your products in our warehouse.
3. When a customer places an order, SnapCart picks, packs, and ships the item.
4. FBS products are eligible for same-day and next-day shipping.
5. SnapCart handles customer service and returns for FBS orders.

**FBS Benefits:**
- Products display a "Fulfilled by SnapCart" badge, increasing customer trust and conversion rates.
- Eligible for SnapCart's free-shipping promotions.
- Reduced seller workload — no need to manage individual shipments.

## Getting Paid

- Seller payouts are deposited every **14 days** into your registered U.S. bank account via ACH transfer.
- The payout reflects total sales minus referral fees, processing fees, FBS fees (if applicable), and any refund deductions.
- Detailed payout reports are available in the Seller Central Finance dashboard.

## Seller Performance Standards

SnapCart holds all marketplace sellers to the following standards:

| Metric | Target | Consequence of Non-Compliance |
|---|---|---|
| Order defect rate (ODR) | < 1% | Account warning at 1%, suspension at 2% |
| Late shipment rate | < 4% | Warning at 4%, listing suppression at 6% |
| Pre-fulfillment cancel rate | < 2.5% | Warning at 2.5%, suspension at 5% |
| Customer response time | < 24 hours | Metrics impact on seller score |
| Policy violation (counterfeit, prohibited items) | Zero tolerance | Immediate account termination |

## Prohibited Products

The following may **not** be sold on SnapCart Marketplace:

- Counterfeit, replica, or unauthorized goods
- Weapons, ammunition, and explosives
- Controlled substances, drugs, and drug paraphernalia
- Tobacco and nicotine products
- Live animals
- Recalled products
- Stolen property
- Products that violate intellectual property rights

A full list is available in the SnapCart Seller Policy Handbook accessible from Seller Central.

# Page 4: Handling Wrong, Defective, or Missing Items

## You Received the Wrong Item

If the item delivered does not match what you ordered (wrong product, wrong color, wrong size, wrong variant):

### Step-by-Step Resolution

1. **Do not discard the wrong item or its packaging.** You will need to return it.
2. **Document the issue.** Take clear photos of:
   - The shipping label on the outer package (showing the order ID)
   - The item you received (showing how it differs from what you ordered)
   - The packing slip inside the box (if any)
3. **Contact SnapCart support** within 7 days of delivery via the Customer Service page. Select "Order issue" as the category and include:
   - Your order ID
   - A description of the discrepancy (e.g., "Ordered black headphones, received white")
   - The photos from Step 2
4. **SnapCart will respond within 24 hours** with one of the following resolutions:
   - **Immediate re-shipment** of the correct item via express shipping at no cost, plus a prepaid return label for the wrong item.
   - **Full refund** if you prefer not to wait for the correct item, or if it is out of stock.
5. **Return the wrong item** using the prepaid label within 14 days. You will not be charged if the return is delayed, but the label expires after 30 days.

> **Note:** In some cases for low-value items (under $15), SnapCart may issue a refund or send the correct item without requiring you to return the wrong one.

## You Received a Defective or Damaged Item

If the item you received is defective (does not function as described) or was damaged during shipping:

### Step-by-Step Resolution

1. **Stop using the item** if it presents a safety hazard (e.g., cracked electronics, broken glass).
2. **Document the damage thoroughly.** Take photos and/or video of:
   - The outer shipping box (showing any visible damage to the box itself)
   - The inner packaging and protective materials
   - The defective/damaged item from multiple angles
   - The defect in action (e.g., a video showing a device not powering on, a photo of a torn seam)
3. **Contact SnapCart support** within 30 days of delivery. Select "Order issue" and specify "Damaged or defective item." Include:
   - Your order ID
   - A description of the defect or damage
   - Photos/video from Step 2
4. **Resolution options:**
   - **Replacement:** A new unit shipped via express at no cost. You receive a prepaid label to return the defective item.
   - **Full refund:** Refund to your original payment method. Prepaid return label provided.
   - **Partial refund (keep the item):** In some cases (cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality), SnapCart may offer a partial refund of 15%–40% off the item price if you prefer to keep the item as-is.

### Manufacturer vs. Shipping Damage

- **Manufacturer defect** (e.g., dead pixels on a monitor, a sweater with a factory hole): SnapCart handles the full return/replacement process. You do not need to contact the manufacturer.
- **Shipping damage** (e.g., crushed box, shattered item): SnapCart files the carrier damage claim on your behalf. You do not need to contact the carrier directly.

## Items Missing from Your Order

If your order contained multiple items but one or more are missing from the delivered package:

1. **Check for multiple shipments.** Orders with items from different warehouses or categories may ship in separate packages with separate tracking numbers. Check your shipping confirmation email for multiple tracking numbers.
2. **Check the packing slip.** If the packing slip lists all ordered items but some are not in the box, contact SnapCart support.
3. **Contact support** within 7 days of delivery with:
   - Your order ID
   - Which item(s) are missing
   - A photo of the packing slip and the contents of the package
4. SnapCart will either **re-ship the missing item(s)** or **issue a refund** for the missing items.

## Package Marked as Delivered But Not Received

1. **Wait 24 hours.** Occasionally, carriers mark a package delivered before the final drop-off.
2. **Check around your property** — behind gates, under doormats, with neighbors, at building mailrooms.
3. **Review the delivery photo** (if available) in your tracking details.
4. If still not found after 24 hours, contact SnapCart support. We will initiate a carrier investigation. See the "Package Theft and Porch Piracy" section on Page 2 for further steps.

# Page 5: Escalating Complaints & Customer Grievance Redressal

## SnapCart's Three-Tier Complaint Resolution System

SnapCart is committed to resolving every customer issue fairly and promptly. Our complaint resolution process has three tiers, each with increasing authority to address your concern.

### Tier 1: Frontline Customer Support

- **How to reach them:** Submit a request via the Customer Service page on snapcart.com, or email support@snapcart.com.
- **Response time:** Within 24 hours (usually faster during business hours, Mon–Fri 8 AM – 8 PM ET).
- **What they can do:**
  - Process returns, replacements, and refunds
  - Update shipping addresses and order details
  - Apply courtesy credits and promotional adjustments
  - Provide order status updates and tracking assistance
  - Resolve most standard issues in a single interaction

### Tier 2: Senior Support Specialist

If Tier 1 support was unable to resolve your issue satisfactorily, you may escalate to a Senior Specialist.

- **How to escalate:** Reply to your existing support ticket and include the phrase **"I would like to escalate this issue"** in your message. Alternatively, email escalations@snapcart.com with your original case number.
- **Response time:** Within 48 hours. A Senior Specialist will be assigned to your case and will provide a direct contact reference.
- **What they can do:**
  - Override standard policy when circumstances warrant (e.g., extending a return window for documented medical or travel reasons)
  - Issue goodwill compensation (store credit, upgraded shipping on future orders)
  - Coordinate with warehouse, logistics, and seller teams directly
  - Handle complex multi-issue cases

### Tier 3: Customer Grievance Officer

If your issue remains unresolved after Tier 2 review, you may file a formal grievance.

- **How to file:** Email grievance@snapcart.com with the subject line "Formal Grievance — Case #[your case number]." Include a summary of the issue, all previous correspondence, and your desired resolution.
- **Response time:** Acknowledgment within 24 hours; full resolution within 7 business days.
- **What they can do:**
  - Conduct a full investigation with access to all internal records
  - Issue exceptions to company policy where warranted
  - Provide a written explanation of the final resolution
  - Coordinate with legal and compliance teams if necessary

## What to Include in a Complaint

For the fastest resolution at any tier, include:

- **Order ID(s)** related to the issue.
- **Date of purchase and delivery.**
- **Detailed description** of the problem, including what happened and what you expected.
- **Supporting evidence:** Photos, screenshots of error messages, bank statements showing incorrect charges, etc.
- **Previous correspondence:** If escalating, include or reference your prior case number and the resolution offered (and why it was unsatisfactory).
- **Your desired outcome:** Be specific — "I would like a full refund" or "I would like a replacement shipped via next-day delivery."

## Social Media and Public Reviews

While SnapCart monitors social media mentions and review platforms, **the fastest way to resolve an issue is through the official support channels listed above.** Posting on social media may get a response from our community team, but they will redirect you to the support channels for resolution.

## External Dispute Resolution

If you have exhausted all three tiers of SnapCart's internal complaint process and remain unsatisfied:

- **Credit card chargeback:** You may file a dispute with your credit card issuer. Note that SnapCart cooperates fully with chargeback investigations and provides documentation of our resolution attempts.
- **Better Business Bureau (BBB):** You may file a complaint at bbb.org. SnapCart responds to all BBB complaints within 14 days.
- **State Attorney General:** Consumers may file a complaint with their state attorney general's consumer protection division.
- **Federal Trade Commission (FTC):** For issues related to deceptive practices, consumers may file a report at reportfraud.ftc.gov.

## Accepted Payment Methods — Detailed Guide

## Common Payment Errors and How to Fix Them

### "Payment declined"

**Possible causes and fixes:**

1. **Insufficient funds.** Check your available balance or credit limit with your bank.
2. **Incorrect card details.** Verify the card number, expiration date, CVV, and billing address match what your bank has on file.
3. **Bank fraud block.** Your bank may have flagged the transaction as suspicious. Call your bank, authorize the charge, and retry.
4. **Expired card.** Update your card details in Account Settings if your card has been renewed.
5. **International card restrictions.** Some non-U.S. issued cards block online purchases from U.S. merchants by default. Contact your card issuer to enable international transactions.

### "Payment processing error"

This is a temporary system issue. Wait 5–10 minutes and retry. If the problem persists for more than 30 minutes, contact SnapCart support.

### "Duplicate charge" on your bank statement

SnapCart does **not** charge your card until the order ships. What may appear as a duplicate charge is often:

- An **authorization hold** (pending charge) plus the **actual charge**. The authorization hold drops off automatically within 3–7 business days.
- If you see two completed (posted) charges for the same order, contact SnapCart support with your order ID and a screenshot of your statement. We will refund the duplicate within 1 business day.

### "Amount charged differs from order total"

This can happen when:

- A promotional code was applied after the authorization hold was placed. The hold reflects the pre-discount amount; the actual charge reflects the discounted amount.
- Tax calculation was adjusted between authorization and shipment (rare, due to address corrections).
- An item in a multi-item order was out of stock and removed. You are only charged for items that ship.

## Pre-Authorization Holds

When you place an order, SnapCart places a temporary **authorization hold** on your payment method to verify funds. Key facts:

- The hold is **not a charge**. It reserves the funds but does not transfer money.
- The hold is replaced by the actual charge when the order ships.
- If the order is cancelled before shipping, the hold is released. This can take **1–7 business days** to disappear from your statement, depending on your bank.
- Debit cards: Holds may reduce your available balance even though the money has not been withdrawn. Your bank can confirm the hold will release.

## Tax Charges

- SnapCart collects applicable **state and local sales tax** based on the shipping address.
- Tax is calculated at checkout and shown in the order summary before you place the order.
- Some states exempt certain product categories (e.g., clothing in Pennsylvania, books in New York). These exemptions are applied automatically.
- If you are tax-exempt (e.g., a nonprofit organization or reseller), email tax-exempt@snapcart.com with your exemption certificate to have your account flagged for tax-free purchasing.

## Promotional Codes and Gift Cards

- **One promotional code per order.** Codes cannot be stacked.
- Promotional codes may have minimum purchase requirements, category restrictions, and expiration dates. These are displayed at the time the code is issued.
- SnapCart Gift Cards do not expire and have no maintenance fees.
- Gift card balances can be checked at snapcart.com/gift-card-balance.
- Gift cards cannot be redeemed for cash except where required by applicable state law.

# Page 7: Account Security, Privacy & Data Protection

## Keeping Your SnapCart Account Secure

### Password Best Practices

- Use a **unique password** that you do not use on any other website.
- Minimum length is 6 characters, but we strongly recommend **12 or more characters** with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
- Do not include personal information (name, birthday, email) in your password.
- Consider using a **password manager** (e.g., 1Password, Bitwarden, Apple Keychain) to generate and store strong passwords.

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

SnapCart supports two-factor authentication for added security. When enabled:

- After entering your password, you will be prompted for a one-time code.
- Codes are delivered via **email** or an **authenticator app** (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.).
- To enable 2FA, go to Account Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication.

### Recognizing Phishing Attempts

SnapCart will **never** do the following:

- Ask for your password via email, phone, or chat.
- Send emails asking you to "verify your account" by clicking a link and entering your login credentials.
- Request your full credit card number via email or phone.

If you receive a suspicious email claiming to be from SnapCart:

1. **Do not click any links** in the email.
2. Check the sender's email address. Legitimate SnapCart emails come from @snapcart.com domains only.
3. Forward the suspicious email to phishing@snapcart.com for investigation.
4. If you accidentally clicked a link and entered your credentials, **change your password immediately** and enable 2FA.

### Unauthorized Account Activity

If you notice orders, address changes, or password changes that you did not make:

1. **Change your password immediately** from Account Settings.
2. **Review your order history** for any unauthorized purchases. If found, contact support to cancel and reverse them.
3. **Check your payment methods** — remove any cards or payment methods you do not recognize.
4. Contact SnapCart support and report "Unauthorized account access." Our security team will lock the account temporarily while investigating.

## Data Privacy

### What Data SnapCart Collects

- **Account information:** Name, email, date of birth (optional), password (encrypted).
- **Order information:** Items purchased, shipping addresses, payment methods (last 4 digits and expiration stored; full card numbers are never stored by SnapCart).
- **Browsing data:** Pages viewed, search queries, and interaction patterns (anonymized for analytics).
- **Communication records:** Support tickets, emails, and reviews.

### How Your Data Is Used

- To process and fulfill your orders.
- To provide customer support and resolve issues.
- To improve the shopping experience through personalized recommendations (if notification preferences are enabled).
- To comply with legal obligations (tax reporting, fraud prevention).

### Your Data Rights

- **Access:** You may request a copy of all personal data SnapCart holds about you by emailing privacy@snapcart.com.
- **Correction:** You may update your personal information at any time via Account Settings.

## Product Care Guides

### Electronics

- **Headphones:** Store in the carrying case when not in use. Clean ear pads with a slightly damp cloth — never submerge. Avoid leaving in direct sunlight or hot vehicles, which degrades battery health. Charge to 50% if storing for more than 2 weeks.
- **Monitors:** Clean the screen with a dry microfiber cloth. For stubborn spots, lightly dampen the cloth with distilled water — never spray directly on the screen. Avoid using paper towels, tissues, or household cleaners (Windex, alcohol sprays) as these can damage anti-glare coatings.
- **Mechanical Keyboards:** Use a keycap puller (included with many keyboards) and a can of compressed air to clean between switches every 2–3 months. Avoid eating or drinking directly over the keyboard.
- **Bluetooth Speakers:** Rinse waterproof speakers with fresh water after exposure to saltwater, chlorine, or mud. Dry the charging port completely before charging.

### Clothing

- **Denim Jacket:** Machine wash cold inside-out on a gentle cycle. Hang dry. Avoid bleach. Spot-treat stains with mild soap before washing. Wash infrequently (every 5–10 wears) to preserve color and fit.
- **Merino Wool Sweater:** Hand wash in cool water with a wool-specific detergent (e.g., Eucalan). Lay flat on a towel to dry — never hang, as wool stretches when wet. Fold for storage; do not hang on hangers for extended periods.
- **Running Shoes:** Remove insoles after each use and allow shoes to air dry. Clean by hand with a soft brush and mild soap. Do not machine wash or put in a dryer — heat degrades adhesives and midsole foam.

### Home Goods

- **Ceramic Pour-Over Coffee Set:** Hand wash only. Thermal shock (pouring boiling water into a cold ceramic dripper) can cause cracks — pre-warm the dripper with warm water first. The glass carafe is dishwasher-safe on the top rack.
- **Bamboo Desk Organizer:** Wipe with a dry or slightly damp cloth. Do not soak or submerge in water. Periodically apply a thin coat of food-grade mineral oil to maintain the bamboo's appearance and prevent drying/cracking.
- **Soy Candles:** Trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each lighting. On the first burn, let the candle burn until the wax pool reaches the edges of the jar (2–3 hours) to prevent tunneling. Do not burn for more than 4 hours at a time. Keep away from drafts to prevent uneven burning and soot.

### Books

- **Storage:** Store upright on a shelf, away from direct sunlight (UV causes yellowing and fading). Avoid humid environments (basements, bathrooms) which promote mold and warping.
- **Handling:** Use a bookmark — do not dog-ear pages if you want to preserve condition. Avoid cracking the spine by opening the book to a 120-degree angle maximum.
